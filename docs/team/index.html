About Avatar Engines

Our Approach

We apply high-performance computing and large-scale simulation to solve problems enterprise software hasn’t managed to solve yet. We don’t replace existing systems – we make them powerful through efficient algorithms with the highest degree of automation to make sure our software never creates more work than it reduces while unlocking more value for our customers. Born from financial modeling research, we’re driven by curiosity and hard problems. Our diverse interests allow us to cover everything within a small team, which lets us execute fast and lean.
